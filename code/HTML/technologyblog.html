Impact of technology

Grocery retailing companies are increasingly adopting a number of technological innovations in order to improve the shopping experience of their customers by using those technologies in their stores. Through implementation of such advancements as self-checkout kiosks, mobile applications, and RFID technology, these stores are trying to simplify the processes and offer more convenience to shoppers. Along with self-checkout stations, clients can quickly finish their transactions without any queues and bother at the traditional cash desks. Mobile apps give users functionality such as offers individualized to their purchase history, shopping lists management, as well as ordering groceries for delivery or pickup thus, customers are empowered with more control over their shopping experience. Moreover, the integration of RFID technology facilitates the precise inventory tracking, which in turn, makes sure that the shelves are always sufficient and the cases of the out-of-stock items are eliminated. The improvement in operational efficiency, which is derived from these technological contributions, leaves customers with the smooth and fun shopping experience. (KPS Global , 2024)

Positive Impact

"Technology transforms the traditional shopping experience in supermarkets into a more convenient one that meets our tastes and preferences." After the pandemic, online ordering and delivery have become the key elements for supermarkets in this way customers can shop from home and get their groceries delivered at their doorsteps. This not only makes supermarkets convenient but also allows the supermarkets to sell their goods more broadly. Further the aforementioned point, supermarkets are using technology to customize the shopping experience. Through the customer data analysis, they will be able to comprehend the shopping trends and make individualized suggestions. Retail chains, for instance, are appending augmented reality to generate interactive and immersive shopping experiences that are more fun and engaging, consequently making a visit to the store more interesting and exciting. Online ordering and delivery arrangements have come to be of great significance to supermarkets by the time COVID-19 came. This implies that you do not have to go to the store to get your groceries because you can just shop from home and get them delivered. (Mura, 2023)

Negative Impact

There are some darkside of technology in daily life of people and business. Firstly, technology makes people close with the gadgets and far from people that will hamper the socialization among people in the society. Secondly, it will make us passive as we will be ordering our products by sitting inside the home. Thirdly, there are the groups of cyberattackers that are trying to hack the critial data and information of the people and the business organization.

How technology has affected daily life and business while selling grocery items

Different stages of technology development, from domestic to local businesses, have affected the way grocery industry operates. Owing to online ordering and delivery services, delivery of groceries at your doorstep, convenience is at your finger tip moments. This development delivered both a fuel and time saving of the public. Whereas technology has been the win-win of the grocery stores by virtue of righteous supply networks, inventory management systems and implementation of automated acquaintance procedures, it has had some drawbacks that have been seen by the shoppers. In addition, it has become easier for businesses to tailor the shopping experience to the customersâ€™ preferences through the data they have about their preferences which in turn, results in personalizing promotions and recommendations. Moreover, through providing realistic shopping experience, mobile and online platforms address the needs of larger group of the audience, e.g. those with limited mobility or who may not be able to go shopping in the stores for various reasons. Recent advancements, such as self-checkout kiosks and augmented reality tricks, can as well be the reason for the process becoming interactive and fun. In essence, technological developments have altered the grocery business, making it more accessible, productive and customer oriented.

Conclusions

Finally, we can say that the role of technology in the grocery industry is to make it more convenient, easy and customer-centered by bringing out various changes. Even though all the problems that technology development will cause, the advantages are so huge that they outweigh the disadvantages, which mean a better shopping experience for customers.
